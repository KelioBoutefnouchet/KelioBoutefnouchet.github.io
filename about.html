SKILLS & INTERESTS

Graphic design occupies a significant part of my daily life, as this discipline is undeniably essential to my routine. I apply it both by creating visual universes for other musicians and for my own projects. At the same time, I produce, mix, and perform my musical compositions, burn CDs, and take a close interest in the ecosystem of concerts and event planning. I am also deeply involved in packaging, editorial design, and typography. I have a particular passion for creating collections of art and books centered around visual and musical artistic projects, aiming to push the boundaries of printed objects and deliver immersive reading experiences. Beyond my artistic pursuits, I enjoy hiking and trekking, and I have a keen interest in science communication and popularization

To broaden my horizons, I travel whenever possible to meet people who have a different perspective and culture to get a more comprehensive understanding of the world around me.

Simultaneously, I am self-taught in 3D modeling.

Beyond my artistic interests, I am into athletism (sprint), hiking, trekking and have an interest in sciences.
