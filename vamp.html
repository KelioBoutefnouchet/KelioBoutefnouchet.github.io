➔ LE VERRE À MOITIÉ PLEIN
➔ Designing an identity for the collection including the header design and a layout concept for the interior.
➔ In a world where the news highlighted by the media is particularly anxiety-inducing, some French people stop staying informed, yet this poses a sociological and civic problem. The POSITIVE MEDIA proposes to show the news in a new light, to highlight the positive stories that arise silently every day, without anyone bringing them to your attention. This magazine will be titled "Le Verre À Moitié Plein." Initially, we worked around the concept of "half full," using physical graphic tools to explore different ways of translating this notion.
➔ In response to the client's brief, I proposed a vibrant newspaper that raises awareness that good news is all around us, perhaps even in the objects of our daily lives. Through a playful, bold, and humorous iconography, along with a serious tone, this newspaper will uplift you for the week ahead.
