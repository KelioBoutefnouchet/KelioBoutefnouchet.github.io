➔Insurgence Variable Display Lowercase
➔ A typeface that makes a mark, cuts through, and asserts itself.
Insurgence Display Variable was designed to carry bold messages. Specifically created for headlines, it commands attention in contexts where the message needs to be seen and heard: posters, stickers, banners, websites, and editorial design with a strong voice. Its sharp, angular shapes structure ideas with strength, creating a controlled tension between expressiveness and readability. Every glyph takes a stand. Every word becomes a statement. Thanks to its variable design, Insurgence adapts to the needs of creators, offering extensive typographic flexibility. Its range of styles allows intensity to be adjusted according to graphic requirements while keeping a strong signature. Full support for major European languages, including accented characters and the German Eszett, ensures the typeface can communicate across borders. Insurgence is more than a font. It is a communication tool that demands attention.
