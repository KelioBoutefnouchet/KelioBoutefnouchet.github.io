🡪 About

🡢 Art direction for a rap/hyperpop music project from the label “folld.r” with the theme of authenticity and the will to succeed in an increasingly complex world.
🡢 First, a typographic logo was created, followed by an icon that represents hope, a theme that is very present in the lyrics of the music. The icon can be used on various media, including the cover. The cover was painted to reflect the authenticity of the project. In addition, the album art features 5 different illustrations that correspond to the 5 tracks on the project. The records are all signed and numbered.
🡢The art direction includes: the cover, the logo, typographic titles, the mockup of the CD box, the records, and the cassette, as well as screen printing on textiles.
